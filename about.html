About Us

Sugar&Berries

We are a small, independent and muslim-owned business specializing in sweet treats such as cakes, cupcakes, brownies, cookies, and more! Founded in 2015 by Ajibah binti Muhammad, we strive to provide the sweetest delights to customers in Kuching!

More Details

Our products are freshly baked daily, ensuring the best quality for our customers. We cater for birthdays, weddings, and corporate events. Let us sweeten your moments!

Our Founder

Ajibah binti Muhammad, the founder of Sugar&Berries, was born on 13 April 1992. A graduate of UiTM Samarahan in Accountancy, she began her journey as an entrepreneur in 2015 during her university days, turning her passion for baking into a thriving business.

Our Shop

Our newest shop, Sanggar Manisan is a haven for dessert lovers, offering beautifully crafted treats using the freshest ingredients. From custom cakes to daily delights, we promise an experience filled with warmth and artistry. We sell freshly baked cakes, cupcakes, brownies, and birthday cakes that will for sure brighten up your day! Come visit us now at Metrocity!
